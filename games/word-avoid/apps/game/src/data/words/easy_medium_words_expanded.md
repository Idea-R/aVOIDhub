## DIFFICULTY PROGRESSION NOTES

### Easy Word Characteristics:
- 3-5 letters maximum
- Heavy use of home row keys (ASDF, JKL;)
- Common letter patterns
- High frequency in English
- Minimal pinky/ring finger usage

### Medium Word Characteristics:
- 5-8 letters typically
- Mix of all keyboard rows
- Some challenging combinations but not extreme
- Mix of familiar and technical terms
- Moderate finger stretching required

### Recommended Usage:
- **Easy words**: 50% of beginner level content
- **Medium words**: 70% of intermediate content
- Perfect for building confidence before tackling hard words
- Great for warm-up rounds and accuracy training

### Game Implementation:
- Use easy words for first 2-3 levels
- Gradually introduce medium words
- Mix ratios: 80% easy/20% medium → 60% easy/40% medium → 40% easy/60% medium
- Save hard/extreme words for advanced players only

This collection provides over 800 additional words perfect for building foundational typing skills without overwhelming new players!
